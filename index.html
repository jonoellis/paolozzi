Sir Eduardo Paolozzi
on
Street View

Artist Sir Eduardo Paolozzi was a pioneer of pop art and one of Britain's greatest sculptors. Because of his importance, his works are frequently bought, sold, loaned, and - crucially - relocated. This page details the pieces on outdoor public display around the world and shows them through the medium of Google Street View.

If you know of an outdoor Paolozzi sculpture that's missing, you can submit it here.

A few other Paolozzi sculptures are visible via aerial photography on Google Earth.

The spreadsheet of sculptures on Street View can be found here, and my ongoing research and scupltures visible on Google Earth can be found here. This is not an attempt to document every work by Paolozzi - just a focus on the public sculptures - so if you know of a missing outdoor Paolozzi sculpture, you can submit it here.

Sir Eduardo Paolozzi has no blue plaques on any of his homes or workshops in England and there are no public sculptures in his hometown of Leith (although there is a dilapidated mural on a derelict shop). Many of Paolozzi's commercial and public pieces are no longer available for the public to view. Hopefully this project goes a little way to remembering those that are.

This is a project by Jono Ellis.
