Sir Eduardo Paolozzi
on
Street View

Nobody needs me to write anything about how artist Eduardo Paolozzi was one of the pioneers of pop art, nor that he was one of Britain's greatest sculptors. But because of his importance, his works are bought and sold and loaned and move around and can be a little pesky to track down. This page details the works that I am aware of on outdoor public display around the world and shows them through the medium of Google Street View.
