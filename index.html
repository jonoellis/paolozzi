Artist Sir Eduardo Paolozzi was one of the pioneers of pop art and one of Britain's greatest sculptors. Because of his importance, his works are bought, sold, loaned and move around. This page details the pieces that I am aware of on outdoor public display around the world, and shows them through the medium of Google Street View. Missing one? Let me know here.
